The System

4 Layers.
4 Ways to Win.

Each layer is independent and stackable. A single hole can reward you in all four simultaneously. The bold shot always earns more ‚Äî but the drama layer stays pure.

1
Layer 1
Base Points

Stableford-style scoring. Every player earns base points for their score on each hole, regardless of whether they declare. Birdies jump hard. Eagles jump harder. The jumps are intentional.

2
Layer 2
Declarations

Call your shot before you hit it. A successful declaration unlocks your handicap multiplier. A failed one earns +0.5 consolation for the attempt. No declaration means no multiplier ‚Äî ever.

3
Layer 3
Handicap Multiplier

The same bold shot earns more when it's harder to pull off. High handicappers carry a bigger multiplier because the margin for error is wider and the courage required is greater.

4
Layer 4
Moment Bonuses

Flat bonuses for the plays nobody forgets. Added on top of everything else. Never multiplied. The Hole-in-One is +44 ‚Äî the founding number of the league. Season-changing.

Handicap Head Start

Each player begins with half their handicap (rounded down) added to their base points total only ‚Äî never multiplied. An 18 HCP starts with +9 points. The head start keeps the leaderboard close ‚Äî but a single big moment can flip it instantly.

Layer 2

Declarations

Before any shot, call your intent. Must be declared before leaving the tee box ‚Äî the app locks declarations at 20m GPS movement. No changes after lock. No declaration, no multiplier.

Par 4 Only

Drive the Green

Attempting to reach the green off the tee on a par 4. Driver accuracy and distance both required.

Risk
Par 5 Only

Go for Green in 2

Attempting to reach the par 5 green in two shots. Carry distance, hazards, and wind all factor in.

Risk
Any Hole

Short Cut

A more aggressive line than the intended fairway. Player proposes, group confirms before the shot. If it lands in bounds, the declaration is hit.

Risk
On the Green

1-Putt Par

Declaring you will hole out in one putt from anywhere on the green, any distance. No minimum distance ‚Äî counts from anywhere.

Risk
Admin-Designated Holes

Call Your Score

Before teeing off, declare your exact score. Must match exactly ‚Äî call birdie, make eagle: missed. Call par, make par: hit. Commissioner selects 3‚Äì4 holes per round.

Declaration missed ‚Üí earn base points + +0.5 consolation. No multiplier. Double bogey+ with a miss = 0 base + 0.5 = 0.5 pts total.
